Hanna and Sofia's drinks
Caipirinha

Ingredients
  • 4 cl Rum
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/2 Lime
Preparation

Slice the lime into 1/2-inch rounds, cube them, and muddle them in an Old-Fashioned glass or small tumbler with the sugar. Add a couple of ice cubes. Pour in the rum.

Pina Colada

Ingredients
  • 4 cl Rum
  • 1 dl Coconut milk
  • 1 dl Pineapple juice
Preparation

Mix with crushed ice until smooth. Pour into chilled glass, garnish and serve.

Whiskey Sour

Ingredients
  • 4 cl Bourbon
  • 2 cl Lemon juice
  • 2 cl Simple syrup
Preparation

Shake with ice. Strain into ice-filled old-fashioned glass to serve "on the rocks".
